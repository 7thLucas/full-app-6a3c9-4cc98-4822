# Product Overview

## Identity
- **Working Title**: [To be confirmed during onboarding]
- **Category**: Education & Productivity
- **Platform**: Progressive Web App (PWA) — installable as a shortcut on desktop and mobile, no app store required
- **Design Language**: Minimalist, calming aesthetic with a purple gradient theme; modern, polished, competition-ready
- **Special Mode**: Built-in demo mode for hackathon judges and product evaluators

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Students (secondary school through university) seeking to improve focus and long-term learning outcomes
- **Secondary**: Hackathon judges and evaluators accessing the product through demo mode

## The Problem
Students default to passive study habits — re-reading and highlighting — that produce poor long-term retention. Existing tools are fragmented (a timer here, flashcards there), with no unified system combining proven technique, focus infrastructure, and motivation. Without accountability, community, and visible progress, motivation collapses before lasting habits form. Distractions remain unmanaged, cutting sessions short before real learning happens.

## The Solution
A gamified, science-backed study platform that unites four evidence-based learning techniques with an AI coach, immersive focus tools, and community-driven accountability — delivered as a single polished PWA. Every feature is grounded in learning research and designed to compound over time.

## Core Study Techniques
1. **Feynman Method** — Structured self-explanation sessions to surface and fill knowledge gaps
2. **Spaced Repetition** — Algorithmically timed review cycles that maximize long-term retention
3. **Active Recall** — Retrieval practice through quizzes and flashcards to strengthen memory encoding
4. **Pomodoro Timer** — Time-boxed focus/break cycles to sustain attention without burnout

## Feature Set

### Focus Infrastructure
- **Optional ambient audio** — Background white noise and ambient soundscapes available during any study session
- **Deep Work Mode** — Combines Pomodoro timer, ambient audio, and distraction blocking into one locked-in session
- **Focus Arena** — Timed study challenges where students compete with themselves or peers head-to-head

### AI & Personalization
- **AI-Powered Study Coach** — Recommends the optimal technique based on the student's current task and context
- **Smart Reminders** — Adaptive notifications calibrated to the student's schedule and energy levels
- **Personalized Dashboard** — Displays progress tracking, achievements, and detailed study statistics in one view

### Gamification & Community
- **Ranking systems and streak tracking** — Visible daily progress and competitive rank to reinforce consistency
- **Leaderboards** — Individual and community performance comparisons
- **Community challenges and leagues** — Students earn badges and climb ranks through shared group challenges
- **Peer challenges via Focus Arena** — Head-to-head or group timed study battles

### Analytics
- **Visual retention analytics** — Charts and curves showing how spaced repetition improves recall over time

## Design Principles
- Minimalist, calming aesthetic with purposeful whitespace
- Purple gradient color theme across all UI and branding surfaces
- Smooth, guided onboarding for first-time users
- Competition-ready visual polish at every screen

## Strategic Principles
- **Science-first**: every feature grounded in evidence-based learning research
- **Gamification as infrastructure**: rankings and community are motivational architecture, not decoration
- **Community accountability loops**: sustain long-term engagement well beyond initial onboarding
- **PWA-native**: installable shortcut on desktop and mobile with zero app store friction
- **Demo-ready**: built-in demo mode ensures the full product can be evaluated end-to-end in minutes
- **Hackathon-grade polish**: UI quality and feature completeness are positioned for competition evaluation
