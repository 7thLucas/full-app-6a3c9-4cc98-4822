Unique features to include:
- AI-powered study coach that suggests the best technique based on the student’s current task.
- Smart reminders that adapt to the student’s schedule and energy levels.
- A “Deep Work Mode” combining Pomodoro + white noise + distraction blocking.
- Community challenges and leagues where students can earn badges and climb ranks.
- Visual analytics showing retention improvement through spaced repetition.
- Minimalist, calming UI with purple gradient theme for professionalism.
