The Focus Crisis in Modern Learning

⚠️
The Problem

  • Passive habits produce poor retention — re-reading and highlighting consistently underperform evidence-based methods in learning research.

  • Tool fragmentation breaks routines — scattered timers, flashcard apps, and note tools prevent any coherent study system from forming.

  • Motivation collapses without accountability — no streaks, community, or visible progress means students quit before habits take hold.

✨
The Solution

  • Four evidence-based techniques, one platform — Feynman, spaced repetition, active recall, and Pomodoro, unified in a single coherent experience.

  • AI coach adapts in real time — recommends the optimal technique for each task, eliminating guesswork about how to study.

  • Gamified community sustains momentum — streaks, leagues, and leaderboards create the accountability loop that keeps students coming back.
